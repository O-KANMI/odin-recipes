Pasta

Roman-Style Spaghetti Alla Carrettiera

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces (40g) dried porcini mushrooms
  • 1 cup (235ml) boiling water
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon finely minced fresh parsley leaves
  • 1 (5-ounce; 140g) olive oil-packed tuna
  • 1 pound (450g) dried spaghetti

STEPS

  1. COMBINE 1/4 CUP OLIVE OIL, GARLIC, PEPPER FLAKES AND MINCED PARSLEY
  2. ADD MUSHROOMS AND COOK
  3. ADD TUNA OIL AND BRING TO A SIMMER
  4. INA POT OF SALTED WATER, COOK SPAGHETTI
  5. USING TONGS, TRANSFER SPAGHETTI DIRECTLY INTO SIMMERING SAUCE
  6. TRANSFER TO WARMED PLATES. SERVE RIGHT AWAY
