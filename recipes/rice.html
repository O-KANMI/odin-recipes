Rice

Cooked white rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups warm water
  • 1 cup of white long grain rice
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

STEPS

  1. RINSE THE RICE
  2. REMEMBER THE RATIO
  3. BRING WATER TO A BOIL
  4. ADD YOUR RICE, BUTTER AND A PINCH OF SALT
  5. BRING PAN TO A SIMMER THEN LOWER HEAT
  6. REMOVE FROM HEAT AND LET SIT
